Ink Waterfall 墨瀑

梅兰竹菊。

Combining traditional Chinese ink painting with digital technology, the four plants with traditional Chinese symbolism - plum blossom, orchid, bamboo and chrysanthemum (梅兰竹菊) - are painted in falling splashes of ink and combined with calligraphy and traditional Chinese poetry.

Vimeo Video

"Plum, Orchid, Bamboo and Chrysanthemum (梅兰竹菊) " is a traditional subject in Chinese painting, and since ancient times the Chinese have used these four plants to illustrate the noble character of the gentleman. The splashes of ink are also a classic element of Chinese painting. I have attempted to combine these two elements using digital technology, thus exploring a digital aesthetic approach that combines both traditional meaning and technological sensibility.
