Hanbing Wang

Hanbing Wang is a digital artist and interactive experience designer. Her educational background includes Beijing University and Goldsmiths, University of London, with a research focus on the digital display and interactive experience design of Chinese cultural heritage.

From 2018 to 2022, she studied visual communication design at the School of New Media Art and Design of the Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, where she obtained her bachelor's degree. During this period, she developed her unique creative style, a blend of a deep understanding of Chinese classical aesthetics and modern visual design techniques. This allows her works to express the classic charm of China while incorporating modern aesthetic forms. Currently, she is pursuing a master's degree in computational art at Goldsmiths, University of London, and has further expanded in the fields of physical computing, machine learning, virtual reality, and interactive storytelling. Her creative content includes immersive interactive experience spaces, new media interactive installations, and experimental imagery.

As a cross-disciplinary artist, Hanbing Wang integrates cutting-edge computational technology into her artistic creation, realizing a cross-fusion of computer technology and traditional Chinese art. Whether in art or design, Hanbing Wang demonstrates a unique perspective and technique, revealing an artistic style deeply rooted in tradition yet rich in innovative spirit. She situates artistic critique within the context of Eastern aesthetics, using classical Chinese imagery to create an ambiguous poetic atmosphere, expressing a deep understanding of Chinese culture and a profound homage to traditional aesthetics.
